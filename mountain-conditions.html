Mountain conditions

This page provides a collection of webcameras from various sources and weather forecasts from yr.no for some of the most popular alpine/mountain areas in southern Norway. The page is under development.

List of available areas:

  • Jotunheimen - Sognefjellet
  • Jotunheimen - Valdresflye
  • Trollheimen
  • Rondane
  • Dovrefjell
  • Folldal
  • Romsdal

Other useful tools:

  • Varsom - avalanche forecast
  • regObs - avalanche observations
  • yr.no - weather forecast
  • senorge.no - snow conditions
  • geodata.ngi.no - slopes and topography (bratthetskart)
